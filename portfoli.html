Research Experience

  • The determination of volatile organic compounds from breath related to emotions induced by activities using GC-MS
    2021 - 2022
    About the relationship between volatile organic compounds (VOCs) from breath and emotions induced by different activities
    • Objective : Finding the ralationship of VOCS from breath to analyse emotions which induced by activities
      (The face masks collected from volunteers wearing them during activity)
      • listening to a series of easy-listening music (representing an activity that induced a positive emotion)
      • doing speed typing test (representing an activity that induced a negative emotion)
    • Conclusion
      • These VOCs might relate to the positive emotion : 4-(1,1-Dimethylpropyl) phenol, 3-methyl-tridecane, Dodecane, & Decane
      • These VOCs might relate to the negative emotion : Nonanal, 3-Methyl-undecane, & 7-Methyl-pentadecane
    • Application
      The result obtained from this work could be collected as a database, which may be utilized to develop more accurate devices or tools to assist stress or depressive disorders screening or devices to help understanding emotions of people who lack of abilities to express their emotions properly.
